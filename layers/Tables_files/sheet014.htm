Index half 0x3
Flow status half 0: clear to send consecutive Frame
1: Wait for Next flow-control frame
2: abort the multiframe communication(Buffer overflow)
Block Size 2 how many consecutive frames can be sent at once
 ST min 3 Minimum time gaps between time frames
